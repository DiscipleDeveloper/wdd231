Annual Major Events

Balatong Festival

May 7-10, 2025

Mung beans is the prominent product of San Mateo labeled as "Black Gold". This is the best time to gether with local business and make network to develop entrepeneur's aspirations.

Buwan Ng Wika

August 2025

Buwan ng wika or Month of Language is a month that comemorates Filipino language heritage with different variety of programs such as singing contests, cooking, trade and many more. While many activities involved it's strictly follows the rule of using only Filipino aspects in every category.

National Farmers Day

Jan 22, 2025

Around 43 percent of the San Mateo area is farm. This day is focused to find innovative ways to further the improvement of agriculture systems involving businesses that fit the need of the town.
